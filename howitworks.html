About BetswithBots

Making informed football decisions using Artificial Inteligence

Betswithbots was created as an end-to-end example of using Artificial Inteligence and Machine Learning algorithms to predict Football games.

Data Analytics is a reality in modern sports. Teams across all sports use advanced analytics to recognize their weeknesses and plan informed strategies. Football is such a sport where data analytics has had a great impact. Betswithbots uses a combination of Neural Network and Gradient Boosted Tree algorithms along with historical data from over 10 years to train Machine Learning models that are able to predict future outcomes.

Our models are constantly learning over time, becoming better and better each season. The predictions are offered as a percentage probability that lies in the [0,100] range, e.g. No Goal (54%).

We currently offer 17 major European Leagues and 2 Football Cups, each one having its own dedicated Machine Learning model. Football predictions are made daily and are available for the previous, current and next day.

BetswithBots proprietary services:

  • Game Result Prediction (1-X-2)
  • Under - Over Prediction
  • Goal - No Goal Prediction

BetswithBots generic services:

  • Fixtures
  • League Standings
